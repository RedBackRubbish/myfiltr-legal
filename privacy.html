MyFiltr may allow you to enter or track health-related information (for example, lifestyle inputs, symptoms, metrics, or progress notes). We treat this data as sensitive and aim to minimize collection, access, and retention.

If you purchase a subscription in the iOS App, payments are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may share information only in the following situations:

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. We aim to minimize stored data and delete it when it is no longer needed.

MyFiltr is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
