FAQ

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iPhone: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → MyFiltr → Cancel.

I was charged and I want a refund.

Apple handles refunds for In-App Purchases. You can request a refund via Apple’s “Report a Problem” flow in your Apple account.

The app is crashing / not working.

Please email support with: device model, iOS version, app version, and what you were doing right before it happened.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email support@myfiltr.app with the subject “Account Deletion Request - MyFiltr”. We will verify your request and delete your account and associated data where applicable, subject to legal and security requirements.
